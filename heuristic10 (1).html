10. Help and Documentation

Short Summary

The tenth usability heuristic, Help and Documentation, means that even though a well-designed system should be easy to use without instructions, users should still have access to clear and helpful support when needed. This can include guides, FAQs, tooltips, or a search feature that helps users find answers quickly. The information should be easy to understand, well-organised, and focused on the user’s task. For example, an app might include a “Help” section that explains common actions step by step. This heuristic ensures users never feel stuck and can always find the guidance they need to complete their goals confidently.
