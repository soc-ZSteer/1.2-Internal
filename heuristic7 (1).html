7. Flexibility and Efficiency of Use

Short Summary

The seventh usability heuristic, Flexibility and Efficiency of Use, means that a system should cater to both new and experienced users by offering different ways to complete tasks. Beginners might prefer simple, guided steps, while experienced users often want shortcuts or faster methods. For example, keyboard shortcuts, custom settings, or predictive text can help frequent users work more efficiently without slowing down those who are still learning. This heuristic focuses on making the system adaptable and efficient for everyone, improving overall usability and satisfaction.
